= What's New

QALIPSIS 2.0 is the initial product release.

QALIPSIS 2.0 is designed with the scalability and simplicity required to perform load-tests and performance verification on both distributed and stand-alone systems

With QALIPSIS 2.0:

* You can create scenarios with different branches and running with many minions.
* You can use its many native steps and plugins to fire load on your system and feed QALIPSIS with data to verify load strength.

QALIPSIS 1.0 runs standalone and distributed. A history of events and metrics can be logged or saved in Elasticsearch, TimescaleDB and InfluxDB. Plugins for other tools will be available in future releases.

Future releases of QALIPSIS will include more plugins, more steps for load injection and verifications as well as other improvements.
